Season pass

A season pass is valid for 12 months from the date of issue. The benefits you get as a season card holder are the benefits that apply at the time your season pass takes effect. Below you can see all your benefits as a season card holder and terms of sale.

Tickets

To avoid queues and congestion in the garden, christmas is only available at the Zoo with pre-purchased tickets. You can buy tickets in our webshop.

Fancy buffet

The Christmas buffet is served twice every evening at 17-18.30 and at 18.45-20.15, we recommend you to book a table in advance.
